Simple Grilled Cheese

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or your favorite)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoons mayo

Instructions

  1. Spread butter evenly on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Place one slice, butter side down, on a heated skillet.
  3. Add mayo & cheese on top, then cover with the second slice (butter side up).
  4. Cook until golden brown on one side, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until both sides are golden and cheese is melted.

Enjoy your crispy, gooey grilled cheese sandwich!
